What is Collect Med?
Collect Med is a complete medical claim denial management solution in an ASP application.
How does Collect Med Work?
Collect Med analyzes the ERN (Electronic Remittance Notice or ANSI 835 files) and assigns denied claims to personnel for reprocessing. Collect Med uses Microsoft Internet Explorer to access the system.
Where do I find my ANSI 835 files?
ANSI 835 files are usually located in a folder on your PC or network. The location varies. Our knowledgeable support staff will assist you in locating them and uploading them to your Collect Med central database.
Do I need any special equipment of software to use Collect Med?
Collect Med is a ASP software that runs on any PC that has internet assess and runs Microsoft Internet Explorer Web Browser.

How will Collect Med benefit my company?
Payers have developed complicated payment methods and are using sophisticated technology to assist in denying, rejecting and underpaying claims. The result is that it is becoming increasingly difficult and costly to determine if appropriate payment is made for the services rendered. The revenue cycle has become so complex and the resulting transactions so massive, that it is difficult, if not impossible, for management to maintain an acceptable match between services provided and payments for these services. Collect Med has developed an application that aid health care providers in managing there denied claim processes. This application is deployed through a secure Internet connection, is simple to use and increases the productivity and accuracy of the account receivable staff resulting in an improvement in the companies cash position.
